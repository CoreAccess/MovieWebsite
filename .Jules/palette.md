## 2026-03-16 - Add explicit form labels in Contact page
**Learning:** Found that basic form accessibility (matching `for` and `id` attributes on labels and inputs) was missing on the contact page. This is a common pattern to watch out for in other forms across the application.
**Action:** Always ensure `for` and `id` attributes are explicitly paired in forms to improve screen reader compatibility and allow users to click labels to focus inputs.
