About FilmGap

A next-generation metadata platform optimized for the AI era.

Our Mission

FilmGap is built with a "Data-First" philosophy. We believe that entertainment discovery should be powered by clean, structured, and accessible metadata. Our platform transforms raw cinematic data into a rich, interconnected graph of movies, shows, and creators.

The Identity of Film

By adhering to strict Schema.org standards and providing real-time JSON-LD payloads, FilmGap ensures that every film has a persistent, readable digital identity. We are the bridge between classic cinema and modern AI-driven discovery.
